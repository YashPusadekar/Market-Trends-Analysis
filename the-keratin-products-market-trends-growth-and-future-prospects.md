<h3>What Are Keratin Products?</h3>
<p><a href="https://www.sphericalinsights.com/reports/dry-shampoo-market" target="_blank">Keratin products</a>&nbsp;are hair care solutions infused with keratin protein, designed to nourish and restore the hair shaft. These products include shampoos, conditioners, treatments, hair masks, serums, oils, and styling products that aim to strengthen, smooth, and hydrate the hair. Keratin is used in both at-home treatments and professional salon procedures, with the most popular being keratin hair straightening treatments that help combat frizz and enhance shine.</p>
<h3>Market Overview</h3>
<p>The global keratin products market is experiencing a surge in demand due to growing awareness of the benefits of keratin for hair health and its widespread use in various personal care products. The market includes a wide range of products for consumers, ranging from budget-friendly options to high-end, salon-grade treatments. The demand for keratin hair care products is not limited to one region but is seen across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America, with particular growth in emerging markets.</p>
