<h4>What is Sustainable Aviation Fuel?</h4>
<p><a href="https://www.sphericalinsights.com/reports/sustainable-aviation-fuel-market" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sustainable Aviation Fuel Market</a>&nbsp;is a biofuel used to power aircraft that has similar properties to conventional jet fuel but with a significantly lower carbon footprint. SAF can be produced from various feedstocks, including:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Waste oils</strong>&nbsp;from biological sources</li>
<li><strong>Agricultural residues</strong></li>
<li><strong>Municipal solid waste</strong></li>
<li><strong>Algae and other biological sources</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>These feedstocks are converted into fuel through processes that emit fewer greenhouse gases compared to traditional jet fuel production.</p>
